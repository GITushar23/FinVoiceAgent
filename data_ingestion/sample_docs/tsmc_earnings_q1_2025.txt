TSMC Reports Strong Q1 2025 Results Amidst AI Demand

Hsinchu, Taiwan – April 15, 2025 – Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company (TSMC) today announced its financial results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2025. The company posted consolidated revenue of NT$600 billion, an increase of 15% year-over-year. Net income reached NT$250 billion, beating analyst expectations by 4%. This growth was primarily driven by strong demand for advanced nodes from AI and HPC applications. TSMC's 3nm technology contributed significantly to the revenue. Management expressed optimism for the upcoming quarter, citing continued robust demand signals.
